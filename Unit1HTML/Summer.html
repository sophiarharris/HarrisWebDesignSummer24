Fourth of July

Every year, my country club hosts a huge Fourth of July Celebration. Last summer, I invited my best friend, Lauren. First, we went swimming in the pool for a while, then we ate a delicious barbecue dinner. Next, we explored the different tents, where we got balloon animals and glitter tattoos. We met up with some of my other friends, and went to the dance floor, where we had fun for hours. Once it was dark enough, it was time for the fireworks. The show was spectacular, a great ending to a great evening with my favorite people.
