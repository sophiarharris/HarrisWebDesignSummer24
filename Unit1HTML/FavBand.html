Coldplay

Coldplay is my favorite band because they have so much amazing music that is so unique from other artists. My favorite of their songs is "Fix You" because I really like the message and the bass guitar solo by my favorite member, Guy Berryman, is really awesome. However, Coldplay has so many other hits, such as "Viva La Vida", "Yellow", "A Sky Full of Stars" , "The Scientist", just to name a few. In conclusion, Coldplay is my favorite band and I believe that they will go down in history as one of the greatest musical groups of the twenty-first century.
