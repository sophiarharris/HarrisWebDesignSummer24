Full Swing Golf Conference

Welcome to the website of the HSGA's most popular event!

Full Swing is the place where high school golfers from all across the nation come together to take part in a sports convention like no other. With speeches from leading professionals such as Bryson DeChambeau and Nelly Korda, as well as opportunities to get to know college coaches, attending Full Swing in Detroit, Michigan August 16-17 is a must.

The HSGA is an organization whose goal is to advance the game of golf among high school students in the United States. That is why our name stands for "High School Golf Association"! From working directly with schools to create new teams, to planning this nation-wide convention, HSGA does it all when it comes to high school golf.
