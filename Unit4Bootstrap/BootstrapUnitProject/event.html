    Venue Details:
    Huntington Place

    All day one activities will take place on level two of Huntington Place, pictured in the map.

    Huntington Place is one of the largest convention centers in the world. Located in Downtown Detroit, it has been the host of several prestigious events such as the North American International Auto Show. Huntington Place features beautiful views of the Detroit River, making it a great place to showcase the beauty of the city at Full Swing.
