Individual Practice Guidance

  • Practice at the range to build muscle memory
  • At least sixty percent of your practice should be on short game, which can be done at home or at a nearby golf course
  • Practice on the course to gain course management skills
    • Join Youth On Course to play most courses for only five dollars
  • See a swing coach such as Dan Thomas of Fox Hills Learning Center (click on the image for more information about Dan Thomas)
