2024 Tryout Information

Varsity and J.V. Tryouts will take place at multiple courses.

Specific dates and times:

  • Monday, August 12-
    • 3:30-5:00 at Pheasant Run Golf Club Driving Range
    • Bring twelve dollars for range balls
  • Tuesday, August 13-
    • 2:00-4:30 at Dearborn Hills Golf Course
    • Bring seven dollars for a green fees of a nine-hole round

Total Tryout Cost: Nineteen Dollars

Players without a filled out PlanetHS profile will not be allowed to try out!

There will be a mandatory parent/player meeting from 6:30- 7:30 P.M. on Tuesday, August 13 in the high school APR. All players must attend with at least one parent. The team roster will be released at this meeting.

If you are interested in trying out, please email Coach Dan Berry at berryd@divinechildhighschool.org
